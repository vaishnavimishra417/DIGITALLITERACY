SOCIAL MEDIA DO’s AND DON’Ts

DO’s:
1. Use strong and unique passwords.
2. Keep your profile professional.
3. Think before posting anything online.
4. Respect others’ opinions and privacy.
5. Enable two-factor authentication.

DON’Ts:
1. Do not share personal information publicly.
2. Do not click on unknown links.
3. Do not share OTP or passwords.
4. Do not post offensive or harmful content.
5. Do not trust unknown people online.
